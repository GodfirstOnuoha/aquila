BCHW [Black Camel Hard Woods]

African cultured premium quality hard wood

At Black Camel we are passionate about the beauty and versatility of timber . With decades of experience in teak cultivation and processing, we have established ourselves as West-Africa's leading supplier of high-quality teak and timber products. Whether you're an architect, designer, furniture manufacturer, or an individual looking for premium timber, you've come to the right place.

Our vision is to become the choice supplier of African exotic hardwood and similar resources to diverse worldwide client base.

Our Teak

  1. Sustainable Sourcing: We take pride in our commitment to environmentally responsible practices (backed by awards and certificates to this effect). Our teak is responsibly sourced from sustainably managed plantations certified to meet the highest industry standards.
  2. Premium Quality: We provide the finest of teak wood (First European Quality), known for its exceptional durability, stunning grain patterns, and natural resistance to pests and decay. With renowned versatility of applications from elegant furniture and decking to boat building and architectural applications. Our rigorous quality control processes ensure that every piece of teak we supply meets the highest quality standards.

We provide custom-cut teak to suit a wide range of projects.

Why Choose Us

  1. This is what we love and have been doing for years, having an in-depth understanding of the wood characteristics and how to maximize its potential. We have the expertise and experience, as well as the finest of timbers across west africa.
  2. We have YOU at the center. All we do is geared towards ensuring you find satisfaction. From inquiry to the delivery of your teak wood, we support/are there for you all the way
